Bio

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro GOIH ComM is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Premier League club Manchester United and the Portugal national team.

Achievements

  • 5× Ballon d'Or winner
  • 7× UEFA Team of the Year
  • 4× UEFA Champions League winner
  • 2× UEFA European Championship winner with Portugal
